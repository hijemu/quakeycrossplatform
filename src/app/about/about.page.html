Quakey Quakey

is a public service app for raising earthquake awareness among Filipinos. Parsing PHIVOLCS data, the app displays Philippine earthquakes in the last 24 hours. When there's an earthquake within 300km radius from your location or an earthquake with magnitude of at least 4.0, the app will alert you. Internet access required to use this app.
